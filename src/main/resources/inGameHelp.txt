     --- HELP SCREEN ----
     Valid Commands:
  Move/go(used to Navigate Map)
  Look "Item" (Provides description of item)
  Use "Items"
  Help (Camp Counselor)
  Map(Shows current location on map)
  Search(looks around the room)
  Status(show location/inventory)
  Quit(Give Up)

  Correct Syntax would be:
  -move north/south/east/west
  -use bat
  -look bat
  -map
